Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort has happily provided visitors with wonderful nature experiences for over 30 years! Visitors seeking a unique nature experience with modern amenities return to Pacific Trails Resort year after year to escape the hustle and bustle of the big city.

The Resort was founded by the Muir family in 1991 as a means to share the beautiful Northern California coastline with friends. While many people would enjoy just camping the area, the Muir family realized that camping does not offer a lot of comforts that the modern family is accustomed to. By implementing Luxury Yurts, the Muir family was able to attract people from all walks of life to share in the wonderful nature experiences that the Northern California Coast has to offer.

Mission Statement

We seek to provide special nature experiences to all without sacrificing the comforts of home.

Visitors from all all walks of life are welcome to stay at our resort year-round. Activities offered include hiking, kayaking, bird watching and more!

What We Believe

We firmly believe that nature experiences should be accessible to all without sacrificing the comforts of modern amenities.

We believe that our resort offers a unique experience that combines the raw elements of nature with the modern comforts of home, providing guests with the best of both worlds.

Visit Us

We are open year round to accommodate guests in all seasons. Our Yurts are made to withstand all weather types and ensure the complete comfort of visitors during any time of the year.
